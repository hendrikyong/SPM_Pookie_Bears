How To Play

Intro

You are the mayor of Ngee Ann City, the goal of the game is to build the happiest and most prosperous city, to do that you need to score as many points as possible!

Playing

You start off with 16 coins. Constructing a building cost 1 coin. The game ends when you either run out of coins, or build on all unoccupied squares.

Constructing buildings in a certain manner earns you points. Same for coins, constructing certain buildings earns you coins. For the first building, you can build anywhere in the city. For subsequent constructions, you can only build on squares that are connected to existing buildings. The other building that was not built is discarded.

Types

There are 5 types of buildings
Residential
Commercial
Industry
Park
Road

Point Calculation

  • Residential (R): If it is next to an industry, then it scores 1 point only. Otherwise, it scores 1 point for each adjacent residential or commercial, and 2 points for each adjacent park.
  • Industry (I): Scores 1 point per industry in the city.
  • Commercial (C): Scores 1 point per commercial adjacent to it.
  • Park (O): Scores 1 point per park adjacent to it.
  • Road (*): Scores 1 point per connected road in the same row.

Types

There are only 2 types of buildings that can earn coins:
Commercial
Industry

Score Calculation

  • Industry (I): Each industry generates 1 coin per residential building adjacent to it.
  • Commercial (C): Each commercial generates 1 coin per residential adjacent to it.
